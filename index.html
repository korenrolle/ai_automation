FAQ

The questions serious clients usually ask.

What exactly are you building for clients?

I build custom AI automation systems that act like a digital team. That can include a master orchestrator, specialist agents, long-term memory, lead handling, content systems, email workflows, research agents, CRM follow-up, voice assistants, and business-specific integrations inside platforms like n8n, Supabase, OpenAI, Gmail, and other tools you already use.

Is this just a chatbot?

No. A chatbot answers questions. This system can route tasks, remember context, trigger workflows, hand work to specialist agents, and support real business operations. It is built to function more like an internal operator than a novelty bot.

Who is this best for?

This is best for founders, agencies, service businesses, creators, operators, ecommerce brands, and teams who repeat the same workflows over and over. If you deal with leads, outreach, content, follow-ups, admin work, or fragmented systems, this is for you.

Can you connect it to the tools I already use?

Usually, yes. Most builds are designed around the stack you already have or the stack that best fits your workflow. The exact integrations depend on your tools, access, credentials, and what actions you want automated.

Will the system remember my business and clients?

Yes, if memory is included in your build. I can configure long-term memory using structured storage and vector search so your system can recall preferences, client details, past conversations, brand rules, research, and internal notes.

Can it send emails or do actions automatically?

Yes, but only when those tools are wired in and approved. Some clients want smart drafting only. Others want full execution such as sending emails, updating CRMs, qualifying leads, routing tasks, or triggering downstream automations.

Do you offer voice agents too?

Yes. Voice-enabled builds can be layered on top of the automation system so clients can speak to their assistant, route requests through an orchestrator, and trigger specialist workflows behind the scenes.

How custom is this?

Highly custom. I do not sell generic fluff wrapped in AI language. Your workflow, your offer, your bottlenecks, your tone, and your customer journey shape the architecture.

How long does a build take?

That depends on scope. A lean MVP can move quickly. Larger systems with multiple agents, memory, integrations, dashboards, and QA take longer. Every build starts with architecture so the system is useful before it becomes fancy.

What do you need from me to start?

Usually: your business goal, current workflow, pain points, desired outcomes, must-have integrations, and access to any platforms the build needs to touch. I can build around missing pieces, but some actions require your credentials and approvals.
